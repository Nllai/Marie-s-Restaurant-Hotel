Getting Here

Newfoundland is the most easterly province in Canada. Travel by ferry to Port aux Basques or Argentia. Air travel to one of four airports: Stephenville, Dear Lake, Gander, or St. John's. Deer Lake being the closest airport (approx 130km or 78 mi)

We are located in the Green Bay area. Travel the TransCanada Highway to Highway 390 taking you to the scenic area of Green Bay. Travel 12km (7mi) to the Town of Springdale.

As you enter Springdale, we are 3km from the "Springdale Welcomes You" sign at 63 Little Bay Rd.

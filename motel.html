House Rules

Upon booking your room and providing us with your credit card number and expiry date in lieu of guarantee, your reservation will be confirmed. Any arrival after or departure before the required dates will be considered as cancellation.

In general, there are no penalties for cancellations or modifications made 48 hours prior on your scheduled arrival date.

If you plan to modify/cancel your reservation, please notify us via our contact form, by calling us at 1 (709) 673-3894 or by sending us an e-mail at bookings@mariesmotel.com

If you cancel or modify your reservation after the required cancellation or modification deadline, you will not be refunded.
